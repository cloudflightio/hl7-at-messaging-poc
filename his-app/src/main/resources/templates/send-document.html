What happens next?
  1. Your PDF is encoded as base64 in a FHIR DocumentReference
  2. An ATMessagingBundle is created with MessageHeader, Endpoints, Practitioner, Patient, and DocumentReference
  3. The message is sent via Matrix protocol to the GP room
  4. GP application receives and stores the message
  5. GP can view and download the PDF in their inbox
FHIR Message Structure
Bundle (type: message)
├── MessageHeader
│   ├── event: document
│   ├── definition: ATMessagingDocumentMessageDefinition
│   ├── source: HIS Endpoint
│   └── destination: GP Endpoint + Receiver
├── Endpoint (HIS)
├── Endpoint (GP)
├── Practitioner (Receiver)
├── Patient
└── DocumentReference
    └── content (PDF, base64)
